About

I am a Computing Science student at the University of East Anglia. My interests span across a range of topics in computer science, however im particulary interested in seeing how we can shift security left in software development to prevent bottlenecks further in development.

I am currently working on a project specialising on occlusion in the field of computer vision, particularly in the domain of feed-forward transformer based reconstruction paradigms working with professor Cheng Wang.

I am passionate about building practical systems and exploring how modern deep learning techniques can be applied to real-world problems in computer vision and beyond.

Research

Object-Centric Learning for 3D/4D Reconstruction

Investigating how Object-Centric Learning can make 3D and 4D reconstruction pipelines more efficient. This work explores the intersection of OCL methods with geometric vision models such as CUT3R, aiming to achieve faster and more accurate scene understanding.
